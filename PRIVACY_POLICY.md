# Privacy Policy for Photo Job Manager

**Effective Date:** November 5, 2024  
**Last Updated:** November 5, 2024

## About This Policy

Pinebelt Trophy ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Photo Job Manager mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly
- **Job Information:** Job titles, descriptions, locations, notes, pricing, and status updates
- **Client Information:** Client names, phone numbers, and email addresses that you enter
- **Account Information:** User authentication data through Firebase Authentication
- **Photos:** Images you capture or select for your photo jobs

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the App's features
- **Technical Data:** Error logs and performance metrics to improve the App

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- **Provide Core Functionality:** Enable job management, client tracking, and photo organization
- **Cloud Synchronization:** Sync your data across your devices using Firebase
- **App Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
- **Customer Support:** Respond to your questions and provide technical assistance

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- Photos are stored in your device's secure app directory

### Cloud Storage
- We use Google Firebase for cloud synchronization and user authentication
- Data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
- Firebase complies with industry-standard security practices

### Security Measures
- All data transmission is encrypted
- We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect your information
- Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **DO NOT** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:
- **With Your Consent:** When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
- **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party services (like Firebase) that help us operate the App
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access and Control
- **View Your Data:** Access all your job and client information within the App
- **Edit Information:** Modify or update any data you've entered
- **Delete Data:** Remove individual jobs, clients, or photos at any time
- **Export Data:** Request a copy of your data in a portable format

### Account Management
- **Delete Account:** You can delete your account and associated cloud data
- **Opt-Out:** Disable cloud synchronization to keep data local-only

## Third-Party Services

### Firebase (Google)
We use Google Firebase for:
- User authentication
- Cloud data synchronization
- App analytics

Firebase's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Camera and Photo Library
- **Camera Access:** Used only to capture photos for your jobs
- **Photo Library Access:** Used only to select existing photos for jobs
- **No Automatic Upload:** Photos are only processed when you explicitly add them to jobs

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Local Data:** Remains on your device until you uninstall the App or manually delete it

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Posting the updated policy in the App
- Sending a notification through the App
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
